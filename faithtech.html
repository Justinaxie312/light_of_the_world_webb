Faith and Technology Integration

This educational page explores how faith and technology can work together when guided by the values of “Una ang Ginoo” and “Light of the World.”

Una ang Ginoo in Technology

Placing God first in the use of technology means making ethical and responsible decisions. Faith reminds us that technology should serve humanity and honor God, not cause harm or division.

“Whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God.” — 1 Corinthians 10:31

Being the Light of the World Online

In the digital world, being the “Light of the World” means using technology to spread positivity, truth, and kindness. Our online behavior should reflect our faith and values.

Responsible Use of Technology

  • Practicing honesty and respect online
  • Avoiding harmful and misleading content
  • Using technology to help and educate others

Faith in Digital Action

  • Sharing inspiring and faith-based messages
  • Using technology for community service
  • Balancing screen time with spiritual growth

Educational Reflection

Integrating faith and technology allows individuals to become responsible digital citizens. Guided by “Una ang Ginoo” and “Light of the World,” technology becomes a powerful tool for service, learning, and positive change.

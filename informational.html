Educational Page

This educational page focuses on the values and teachings behind the themes “Una ang Ginoo” and “Light of the World.” These principles guide individuals to live with faith, purpose, and compassion.

Una ang Ginoo

“Una ang Ginoo” teaches us to place God at the center of our lives. By prioritizing faith in our decisions and actions, we learn to live with humility, trust, and moral responsibility.

“Seek first the Kingdom of God and His righteousness, and all these things shall be added unto you.” — Matthew 6:33

Light of the World

Being the “Light of the World” means serving as an example of goodness, kindness, and love to others. Through our words and actions, we reflect God’s light in our community and everyday life.

Living the Light

  • Showing kindness and compassion
  • Helping those in need
  • Practicing honesty and respect

Faith in Action

  • Praying and trusting God
  • Making moral choices
  • Serving the community

Educational Reflection

Understanding the themes “Una ang Ginoo” and “Light of the World” helps learners realize the importance of faith in shaping character. These values encourage individuals to become responsible, compassionate, and faithful members of society.

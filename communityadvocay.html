Community Service: Barangay Clean-Up Drive

Community service plays an important role in building responsibility and cooperation among individuals. One example is the barangay clean-up drive.

  • Organize Volunteers: Gather community members and assign specific tasks to ensure proper coordination.
  • Promote Cleanliness Awareness: Educate residents about waste segregation and proper disposal.
  • Improve the Environment: Clean public areas to create a healthier and safer community.
